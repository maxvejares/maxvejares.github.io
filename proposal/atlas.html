Chilean History Atlas

In Development

An interactive data explorer for Chilean subnational history, currently under construction. The platform will present time series and choropleth maps at the national, provincial, and departmental scales, drawn from censuses, budgets, and other primary sources. A public release will follow once the underlying panel and the cartographic boundaries are stable.
